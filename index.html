About Me

I am a technical writer who explored writing after a short stint in web development. Back in 2014, I was fortunate enough to discover the world of technical communication when I was exposed to API documentation as a web developer. As I was passionate about writing, I learned the tools of the trade for technical writing and became a technical writer in 2015 at Cognizant. In 2016, I was offered the chance to work for Oracle, so I moved away from my hometown. For a little over 4 years, I gathered much-needed experience and rose from a junior technical writer to a senior technical writer. In 2020, I was offered an excellent chance to be involved significantly in creating a brand-new documentation portal at Conviva, so I moved from Oracle to Conviva.

With a positive outlook on everything, I love sharing my knowledge with others enthusiastically. I am dutiful and sincere about my tasks with a keen eye for observation. I love to plan my tasks ahead and am known by my peers as someone who always tries something new and different with my work.
